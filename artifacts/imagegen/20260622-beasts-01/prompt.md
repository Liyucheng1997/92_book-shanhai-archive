# 南山经兽类第一批 2×2 母板

Use case: stylized-concept  
Asset type: 《山海异志》网站异兽图鉴 2×2 批量母板  
Input image 1: 仅作为画风参考，不是编辑目标。继承旧米黄宣纸、高密度手绘墨线、墨绿暗金重彩、传统连环画山水、卷云、回纹边饰和民间异兽谱质感；不要复制参考图中的囚牛、古琴、音符、文字或具体构图。

Create ONE strict 2 columns × 2 rows board on a portrait 4:5 canvas. The four panels are exactly equal portrait 4:5 rectangles. Boundaries are exactly at 50% width and 50% height. Use one thin, clean, uniform aged-paper gutter. No merged cells, no overlap, no irregular collage, no outer title, no row or column label, no crop mark. Keep every creature, tail, ear and decorative border at least 8% inside its own panel. Nothing may cross either midpoint.

Panel order and subjects:

TOP LEFT — 狌狌. A representative beast from the opening of the Nanshan Jing. It resembles a real ape or macaque with clearly WHITE EARS. Show one complete creature in a dynamic transition between low quadrupedal/knuckle walking and human-like upright running. The anatomy must remain plausible for a primate. Mountain forest habitat. Exactly one head, two ears, two arms and two legs. Do not make it a human, orangutan, white-furred ape, or horned monster. The fur is earthy brown; only the ears are white.

TOP RIGHT — 鹿蜀. One complete horse-shaped mountain beast with a clearly WHITE HEAD, natural TIGER STRIPES over its body, and one vivid RED TAIL. Its anatomy is fundamentally equine: horse face, horse torso, four horse legs and hooves. Use curved wind lines and distant responsive birds to imply a song-like call, but do not draw musical notes or instruments. Do not add antlers, deer anatomy, tiger paws, wings, extra tails or armor.

BOTTOM LEFT — 类. One complete long-haired civet/wildcat-like beast. It resembles a 狸: compact feline/civet body, long tail, alert rounded ears, with a distinctive long mane and flowing hair along neck and back (“有髦”). Its face and posture are gracefully androgynous. Use subtle paired and balanced fur markings to suggest “自为牝牡”; do not expose genitals and do not split the body into male/female halves. Exactly one head, four legs and one tail. Do not make it a lion, fox, human hybrid or two-headed beast.

BOTTOM RIGHT — 猼訑. One complete sheep/ibex-like beast standing on mountain rock. CRITICAL COUNTING CONSTRAINTS: exactly NINE separate, clearly visible long tails fanning behind the body; exactly FOUR clearly visible ears around the head, two on each side; exactly TWO eyes located on the UPPER BACK between the shoulder blades, and NO eyes on the face. The face may have closed eye-lines or smooth eyeless sockets. Keep all nine tails fully inside this panel and visibly separable. Four normal sheep legs with cloven hooves. No extra heads, no horns unless small natural sheep horns do not obscure the four ears, no facial eyes, no wings.

Unified style for all four panels: 清末彩绘异兽谱、工笔重彩与传统连环画结合；陈旧米黄宣纸，高密度墨线，粗重外轮廓与极细毛发纹理；墨绿、暗金、赭石、低饱和朱砂矿物色；完整古典山水、松树、瀑布、卷云；墨绿描金回纹和如意云头边饰。四格必须像同一位画师在同一册书中完成，纸色、墨线密度、饱和度、边框粗细、视角和光线一致。每格仅有一只全身异兽，主体约占格内高度 70%，均为三分之二侧面。

Absolutely no text, Chinese characters, Latin letters, numbers, seals, labels, UI, watermark, musical notes, modern objects, anime, game-card rendering, photography, glossy 3D, neon, magical circles, particles, gore, malformed anatomy, duplicate heads, cropped bodies, or elements crossing panel boundaries.
